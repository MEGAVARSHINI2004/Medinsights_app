About MedInsights

Our Mission

MedInsights aims to make skin cancer detection more accessible through artificial intelligence. Our platform uses advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze skin lesions and provide preliminary assessments.

How It Works

  1. Upload a clear image of the skin lesion
  2. Analysis using our AI model trained on thousands of medical images
  3. Results with confidence scores and recommendations
  4. Guidance on next steps and specialist recommendations

Supported Conditions

  • Actinic keratoses (AKIEC)
  • Basal cell carcinoma (BCC)
  • Benign keratosis-like lesions (BKL)
  • Dermatofibroma (DF)
  • Melanoma (MEL)
  • Melanocytic nevi (NV)
  • Vascular lesions (VASC)
  • Normal skin

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This tool is designed for educational and preliminary screening purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice and treatment.
