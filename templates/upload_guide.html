Image Upload Guide

Follow these guidelines for the most accurate AI analysis

Why Image Quality Matters

Our AI analyzes visual patterns in skin lesions. Better image quality means:

  • Higher accuracy (up to 95%+ with good images)
  • Better confidence scores in results
  • More reliable medical guidance
  • Faster analysis without need for re-upload

Do's - Best Practices

Good Lighting

Use natural daylight or bright indoor light

Include Scale

Place coin/ruler next to lesion for size reference

Close-up Shot

Fill frame with lesion for maximum detail

Clean Camera

Wipe camera lens before taking photo

Don'ts - Common Mistakes

Blurry Images

Hold camera steady, use focus

Harsh Shadows

Avoid direct flash causing glare

Filters/Edits

Don't use filters, keep original colors

Low Resolution

Use highest camera quality setting

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare

Clean skin area and position in good light

Step 2: Add Scale

Place coin/ruler for size reference

Step 3: Capture

Take multiple angles if needed

Step 4: Upload

Select the clearest image

Advanced Tips for Better Results

Multiple Angles

Capture lesion from front, side, and if possible, with macro lens for texture details.

Color Accuracy

Use natural white balance. Avoid colored lights that distort skin tones.

Time-lapse

For monitoring, take photos at same time each day with consistent lighting.

Troubleshooting Common Issues
